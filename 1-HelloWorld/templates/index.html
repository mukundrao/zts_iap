Best Teacher of the Universe

Chandrakala G Raju



Chandrakala G Raju, an Assistant Professor with an M.Tech. in Computer Network and Engineering, epitomizes excellence in education. Her six years in the IT industry have uniquely shaped her teaching philosophy, aligning students with industry expectations. Beyond the confines of conventional teaching, she actively contributes to curriculum design, workshops, and industry-driven initiatives. Her disciplined work ethic, gained from the corporate world, reflects in her structured teaching approach. Currently pursuing a Ph.D., she embodies lifelong learning, enriching her pedagogical skills.

As an effective communicator, Chandrakala fosters an engaging learning environment with exemplary problem-solving skills. Her enthusiasm for new initiatives and responsibilities underscores her dynamic teaching style. With an educational background from BMS College of Engineering/VTU and STJIT/Karnataka, she brings a wealth of knowledge to her students. Beyond academia, she serves as a Warden, is involved in non-profit work, and contributes as a jury member for national-level technical programs. Her altruistic nature shines through volunteer work for various organizations, reflecting her optimism and commitment to education. Chandrakala G Raju stands as a beacon of inspiration, making her one of the world's best educators.
